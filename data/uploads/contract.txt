SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENT

This Software Development Agreement (“Agreement”) is made and entered into on January 15th, 2025

1. SCOPE OF WORK
The Developer agrees to design, build, and deploy a custom Inventory Management System (IMS). 
The deliverables include:

   • Role-based authentication

Any additional feature requests not listed above will require a Change Request with updated cost.
